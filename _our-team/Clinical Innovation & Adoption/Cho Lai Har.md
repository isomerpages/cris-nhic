<h1><strong>Senior Executive</strong></h1>
<p>Lai Har holds a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Accounting and Finance from
the University of Greenwich and brings with her over a decade of experience
in governance across the public sector. Prior to joining NHIC, she served
in various government agencies, including the Cyber Security Agency (CSA),
Government Technology Agency of Singapore (GovTech), Smart Nation and Digital
Government Office (SNDGO), and the Agency for Integrated Care (AIC).</p>
<p>Throughout her career, Lai Har has held roles overseeing governance for
Research, Innovation &amp; Enterprise (RIE) initiatives, training grant
fundings, procurement, and financial operations. She has developed a deep
understanding of public sector processes and cross-agency collaboration.</p>
